Events in New Orleans

New Orleans is a great place to go for it's festivals and holiday celebrations. It has approximately 130 festivals per year, which translates to about a festival every three days.

Among the most famous holidays, Mardi Gras has parades and events throughout the entire month, with food, costumes, and elaborate floats. Gather on the streets to catch beads, eat good food, and check out the hard work people put into the parade!
